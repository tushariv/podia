Pre-Sell Products

Let people pre-order your products

Podia’s pre-sell feature lets you set a start date for your products. That means you can collect pre-orders and even start selling your product before it’s finished.

Get your free Podia account

It’s normal to feel like your product isn’t ready. That doesn’t mean you shouldn’t take pre-orders

Selling your product early might feel a bit funny, but pre-ordering is a very common (and successful) way to launch. You can pre-order books, clothes, concert tickets, and even cars. Pre-selling your digital products works the same way.

How does pre-selling work in Podia?

Step 1

Start taking pre-orders when you have your outline

Add your idea to Podia, and set a future start date to allow pre-sales. That way you’ll see if there’s interest in your product before you spend days (or weeks, or months) making it.

Step 2

Build a customer-validated product while sales come in

If people buy your product, you’ve got a winner. Imagine how good it will feel to work on a new project and know it’s something your customers want. Better yet – what if that product could put some cash in your pockets?

Step 3

When your product is ready, Podia handles the delivery

Anyone who pre-ordered your product will automatically receive their purchase on the date you’ve chosen. So you can enjoy launch day without stressing about logistics.
