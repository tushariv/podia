Integrations

Wondering if Podia will integrate with your favorite apps? The answer is probably yes.

Podia connects with hundreds of platforms so you can use more tools in the same place you do everything else.

Get your free account

Connect extra tools and software with Podia

Podia integrations give you the flexibility to run your business exactly the way you want.

Native integrations

Email, analytics, advertising, scheduling - everything can be linked inside Podia

Native Podia integrations let you link other software to your Podia website and products in just a few clicks.

Zapier

Connect Podia to any workflow you like using Zapier

Zapier lets you automate across all your platforms. If you can’t find another way to integrate two tools, chances are Zapier can do it — and Podia connects with Zapier in just a few clicks.

Third party code

Want to connect something else? Podia also supports third-party code snippets

Maybe you have a pop-up tool, countdown timer, or some other software that lets you add something to your website. Just drop your code snippet in Podia, and you’ll be able to use the systems together, no problem.

IFRAMELY EMBEDS

Adding an embed is as easy as copy and paste

Podia uses iFramely to let you embed 1900+ tools into your website — without ever touching a line of code. Paste your link from YouTube, Spotify, Airtable, Google Forms, or hundreds of other systems, and the embed will be on your site when you hit publish.
